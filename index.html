Pikachu

Pikachu is an iconic Electric-type Pokémon known for its yellow fur, red cheeks with electric sacs, and a lightning bolt-shaped tail. It is the mascot of the Pokémon franchise and one of the most recognizable characters worldwide. Pikachu evolves from Pichu and evolves into Raichu. It is known for its agility, cuteness, and its signature move, Thunderbolt.

bulbasur

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon characterized by its blue-green skin, bulb on its back, and large, bright red eyes. It is the first Pokémon in the Pokédex and one of the starter Pokémon in the original Pokémon games. Bulbasaur evolves into Ivysaur and then into Venusaur. It is known for its balanced stats, ability to learn a variety of moves, and its supportive role in battles.

Charizard

Charizard is a dual-type Fire/Flying Pokémon, resembling a large, orange dragon with wings and a flame-tipped tail. It is the final evolution of Charmander, one of the starter Pokémon in the original games. Charizard is known for its immense power, ability to breathe fire, and its iconic appearance. It is a fan-favorite Pokémon and often featured prominently in Pokémon media and merchandise.
